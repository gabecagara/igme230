Iceland x Drone

Skills: Drone Footage, Video Editing

People always say that birds have the best view, because they see the world we live in from up above. I was able to capture what it looks to be 1000ft above the bluest of glaciers and the deepest of mountains when I visited Iceland in 2017. With my Mavic Pro II Drone, I flew my drone and captured a truly new world. Inspired by mind bending scenes in films like Inception and Dr. Strange, I video edited my drone footage with After Effects and created a different Icelandic landscape. The result of that? See above!
